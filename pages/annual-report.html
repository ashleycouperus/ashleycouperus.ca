  • Cheo Annual Report

  • Objective

    CHEO was seeking an Annual Report to showcase their mission, history and achievements of 2014.

  • Challenges

    The challenge was to make the report work in both English and French, without sacrificing the overall layout and design.

  • My Role

    The written content was the sole asset provided. I was in charge of the overall design, animations, typography and editing.

Research

The process started by spending time researching Cheo, possible competitors, previous annual reports, etc. After gathering a respectful amount of research, it was time to create a style tile in order to determine the style of the Annual Report.

Sketches

The next step was to start sketching out possible page layouts. By sketching layouts and possible images to include in the book, it was easy to determine the layout and style that would work best for Cheo.

  • Cheo Annual Report

  • Objective

    This CHEO Annual Report was designed to showcase the company's mission, history and achievements of 2014 in both English and French.

  • Challenges

    The challenge was to make the report work in both English and French without sacrificing the overall layout and design.

  • My Role

    The written content was the sole asset provided. I was in charge of the overall design, animations, typography, and editing.

Research

  • The process started by spending time researching Cheo, possible competitors, previous annual reports, etc. After I gathered a respectful amount of research, I began creating a style tile in order to determine the style of the annual report.

Sketches

  • The next step was to start sketching out possible page layouts. By sketching out layouts and possible images to include in the book, I was able to determine the best layout and style that worked best for Cheo.

Final

  • By following the process I was able to provide Cheo with a successful annual report that suits their needs. I kept a consistant minimilistic approach throughout the report to allow the reader to focus on the written content of the book. No imagery was used in order to petray a childlike image while still having a professional finish. Lastly, the colours were used to provide a warm and comfortable tone as Cheo should be precieved as a welcoming place.

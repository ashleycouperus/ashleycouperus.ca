  • Vitakraft Packaging

  • Objective

    The objective was to redesign the existing packaging to stand out from competitors and increase sales.

  • Challenges

    The challenge was to ensure the packaging isn't harmful to the environment.

  • My Role

    My role was to redesign the product and create the die lines.

Research

  • Vitakraft is a company that is committed to produce the highest quality foods, treats and supplies for animals. Researching the values of Vitakraft, competitors, existing packaging and shelf presence inspired ideas for a strong package design.

Sketches

  • Referencing the existing packaging and trends in the market. Through the research, it was clear that all of their competitors package food in the same style. Straying away from the typical solution, I began sketching new ways to approach the design.

Prototype

The next step was to test the product to see how it would turn out. It took a lot of trial and error to design a successful die line that achieved the proper look and functionality.

Final

Straying away from the typical solution, the packaging now stands out from competitors and increase sales. All of the materials used in producing the package are recyclable to ensure the packaging isn't harmful to the environment.

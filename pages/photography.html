  • Vitakraft Packaging

  • Objective

    The objective was to redesign the existing packaging to stand out from competitors and increase sales.

  • Challenges

    To ensure this packaging isn't harmful to the enivornment, it was designed using recyclable materials.

  • My Role

    My role was to redesign the product, create the dielines, take the exsisting text and transfer onto new packaging.

Research

  • I approached this project by completing lots of research before starting to design. I determined the existing packaging, competitors, trends in the market, target audience, etc.

Sketches

  • Referencing the existing packaging and trends in the market, I wanted the new packaging for Vitakraft to stand out on the self. I realized through my research that all competitors packaged their food in the same style. Straying away from the typical solution, I began sketching new ways to approach the design.

Prototype

The next step was to test the product to see how it would turn out. It tooks lots of trial and error, to design a successful dieline that achieved the look I wanted.

Final

Yogurt Drops are made specifically for rabbits, so the box was designed to replicate a rabbit. By doing this the product successfully stands out on the shelf. Naturally, the owners of the rabbits love animals, so seeing a cute rabbit making up the package design is a sure way to attract them to the product.

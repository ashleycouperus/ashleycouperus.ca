  • Twenty One Pilots Album Cover

  • Objective

    I was in charge of create a Album Cover for Twenty One Pilots album “Blurryface”. The cover must relate to the album and attract costumers.

  • Challenges

    The challenge was editing the photograph so it would display the proper effect. Coming up with a bold image and making it work.

  • My Role

    I was in charge of setting up the subject, photography, editing and production. No additional assets were provided.

Research

I began this project by researching Twenty-One Pilots previous album covers. This gave me an idea of their particular style and how to approach the design. I also, spent time researching other musical artist albums in the same music genre. This allowed me to design an album that will stand out against competitors on the shelf.

Sketches

  • Through my research I was able to come up with sketches that would work as a guide in setting up the photograph.

Final

  • In the end, I was able to produce a bold image to represent Twenty-One Pilots new album. The image creates interest and attracts consumers to want to know more.

  • Quote Book

  • Objective

    The objective of this project was to create a book full of memorable quotes from The Hobbit.

  • Challenges

    It was difficult to create words from different element of nature and get them to work together on the page.

  • My Role

    I was in charge of finding appropriate quotes, creating the words, photography, editing, and printing.

Research

  • To start off this project, I began researching famous quotes from the hobbit movies. I had to determine how to approach designing the book and what would best represent The Hobbit.

Sketches

  • After collecting the quotes I wanted to use for the book, I began exploring possible ways to display them. After spending time sketching out many ideas I was able to narrow in on a strategy.

Final

  • By successfully putting together words from elements of nature, I was able to create a dynamic quote book for The Hobbit. The sticks, rocks, dirt, etc. represent the journey the characters took throughout the movies.

  • Quote Book

  • Objective

    The objective of this project was to create a book full of memorable quotes from The Hobbit.

  • Challenges

    It was difficult to create words from different elements of nature and get them to work together on the page.

  • My Role

    I was in charge of finding appropriate quotes, creating the words, photography, editing, and printing.

Research

To start this project, a lot of time was spent researching famous quotes from the hobbit movies. This was necessary to determine how to approach designing the book, and what would best represent The Hobbit.

Sketches

After collecting the quotes, it was time to explore possible ways to display them. After sketching out many ideas I was able to narrow in on a strategy.

Final

The design of the quote book was influenced by nature along their journey and things they encountered during their travels. Using elements of nature such as rocks, sticks, dirt, leaves, etc. The quote book successfully takes you along the journey with the characters.

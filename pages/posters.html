  • Objective

    The goal of these two posters is to provide information for travelers at Destinations Travel Clinic and Orleans Family Health Clinic.

  • Challenges

    The challenge was ensuring that all the type is readable. It is important that the posters are readable from a distance.

  • My Role

    My role was to create numerous posters from the text I was provided with. I was in charge of the layout and illustrations.

Research

Researching began by collecting all the assets needed and then comparing artwork other designers have created similar to the poster. By doing this, it allowed time to gain layout inspiration and think about what kind of illustrations to include on the poster.

Sketches

Sketching was easy because the style of poster was already determined from the research, and assets I was given. The next step would be sketching out the layouts and illustrations and finalizing the poster designs.

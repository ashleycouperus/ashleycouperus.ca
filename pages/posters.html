  • Objective

    The objective was to create informational posters to be printed and hung around Orleans Family Health & Travel Clinic.

  • Challenges

    The challenge was ensuring that all the type was at a readable size. It is important that they are able to be read from a distance.

  • My Role

    My role was to create numerous posters from the type I was provided with. I was in charge of the layout and illustrations.

Research

I began this project by collecting all the assets I needed. I then started looking at artwork other designers have created similar to the poster I was making. By doing this, I gained inspiration of how I would layout the posters and what kind of illustrations I would include.

Sketches

Sketching was easy because I already knew the style of poster I wanted to create from the research and assets I was given. It was a matter of sketching out the plans I had aready made in my head and finalizing the poster designs.

Final

After creating all of the illustrations and organizing the type for each section I was able to piece it all together in Indesign.
